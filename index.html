About Me

Myself Ritwik Das Gupta,
Dr Drakkon is the pen name of Prof. Ritwik Dasgupta. He was born and grew up in Darjeeling, India. He is currently the associate professor of zoology at Bangabasi Evening College-Kolkata, India. He had an amazing career experience as an exchange research worker with the Institute of Evolutionary Morphology and Ecology, Russian Academy of Sciences Moscow, and then as a Radhakrishnan scholar at Trinity College Oxford University. Drawing on his experiences in these countries and being to some amazing places in the Czech Republic andGermany,he has started spinning stories to entertain his little daughter.
